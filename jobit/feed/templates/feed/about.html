About Us

At JOBIT, we understand that many people are seeking work that can be flexible and fit into their busy lives. As students ourselves, we found that traditional part-time jobs often required a long-term commitment, making it difficult to balance work with our studies and other responsibilities. That's why we created JOBIT, a site where students and anyone else seeking casual, non-permanent work can easily find job posts and get in touch with potential employers.

Our mission is to make it easy for anyone to find work that fits their schedule and lifestyle, without having to make a long-term commitment. Whether you're a student seeking work in your free time, a stay-at-home parent looking for additional income, or anyone else who wants to work without the constraints of a traditional job, JOBIT is here to help.

We're proud to be the creators of JOBIT, and we're committed to providing a platform that connects job seekers with the opportunities they need to succeed. Thank you for choosing JOBIT, and we look forward to helping you find your next temporary job!

Meet the Team

Yuli Suliman, Saar Orus and Adi Rozenberg are the founders of JOBIT.
We are third-year computer science students at Tel Aviv-Yafo Academic College. As students ourselves, we experienced firsthand the challenges of finding work that could fit into our busy schedules. We created JOBIT to provide a solution for students and anyone else seeking flexible, non-permanent work. Our passion for helping others find work that fits their lifestyle and our commitment to providing a top-quality platform have made JOBIT a success.
